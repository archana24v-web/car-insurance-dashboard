📁 Documents to Gather Before Quoting

  • Driver's license (all drivers in household)
  • Vehicle VIN number
  • Current declarations page (if switching)
  • Proof of continuous insurance (3+ years)
  • Social Security Number (for credit check)
  • Vehicle registration / title
  • Odometer reading (for low-mileage discount)
  • Home/renters policy number (for bundle)
  • Student GPA transcript (if applicable)
  • Military ID / DD-214 (if applicable)
  • Current employer info (affinity discounts)
  • Defensive driving course certificate (if any)

❓ Questions to Ask Your Agent

  • "Am I getting the age-25 rate reduction? When exactly does it apply?"
  • "What's the exact % reduction at my renewal after I turn 25?"
  • "Is your telematics program (DriveEasy/Snapshot/Drive Safe) available in Alabama?"
  • "Can I stack telematics + bundle + multi-vehicle discounts simultaneously?"
  • "Do you offer a continuous insurance discount for no coverage gaps?"
  • "Is there an employer affinity or alumni association discount for my company?"
  • "What loyalty milestones unlock additional rate reductions?"
  • "Does my anti-theft system qualify for a discount and by how much?"
  • "Will you provide the new rate in writing after I turn 25?"
  • "What's your claim-free renewal reward after 3 and 5 years?"
  • "If I improve my credit score, how often can I re-rate my policy?"
  • "Are there any discounts I currently qualify for that are not applied to my policy?"
